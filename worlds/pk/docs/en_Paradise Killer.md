# Paradise Killer

## What Does Randomization do to this Game?
- Shuffles nebula drinks, Shinji locations, whiskey bottles, relics, key items, Starlight (upgrades), upgrades, quests and more 

## What is the Goal of Paradise Killer in Archipelago
The goal of Paradise Killer can be:
- Lady Soda Dies 	get X amount of nebula soda drinks send your way
- Lady Whisky Dies 	get X amount of whisky bottles send your way
- Lady Love Dies 	get X amount of level Y relationships

All of which will unlock the Investigators Gun and allow you to finish the game as usual.

## What is Required to Play the Game in Archipelago?
The game is only tested on the steam release of paradise killer on the latest version.

## What Other Adjustments have been made to the Base Game?
- Sphere limiters are in place for each region as definied on the map for the sole reason to prevent a massive sphere 1.

## In-Game Client Commands
- `/connect {address:port} {slotname} {password}`
  - Used to connect to the multiworld. Password is optional
- `/disconnect`
  - Disconnect from the multiworld
- `/deathlink`
  - Toggles deathlink
